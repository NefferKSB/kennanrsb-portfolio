About Me

I love knowing how things work, as a child I was constantly building or taking things apart to get a better understanding of how things functioned. That mentality has stuck with me my entire life. In college I majored in Mechanical Engineering Technology to further that pursuit. Prior to receiving my degree, I worked as an Application Engineer for a few years. During that time, I was introduced to some internally developed gearbox design software. Along with extensively working with this software, I was also tasked with creating a smart quote log and an RMA (return merchandise authorization) log. I accomplished this by using VBA in Excel.

These experiences sparked my interest in programming. While still working as an Applications Engineer, I started following YouTube Udemy, and other various online tutorials covering Web Development topics in my spare time. After around a year of study, I started attending a meetup group where I began to mentor people who where completely new to Web Development. Soon after that I landed my first IT professional position as a ServiceNow Technical Consultant. In that role I used JavaScript extensively as well as AngularJS, HTML, and CSS.

Nearly daily, I research build, or read up on some IT and or programming topic. Outside of these pursuits you may see me on a stage somewhere playing my saxophone.
